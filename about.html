Research Leadership

With over 30 years of experience in finance and technology, I lead the SDRAG research project focused on developing trustworthy AI-assisted financial analytics solutions.

My career spans roles at world-class companies including KPMG, Motorola, HP, HPE, and OpenText, where I've specialized in bridging the gap between engineering and finance teams. This unique perspective drives the SDRAG approach of separating computation from explanation.

The SDRAG project emerged from recognizing that modern AI tools, while fluent in discussing numbers, cannot be trusted to compute financial results consistently. Our structured approach removes arithmetic hallucinations by design.
