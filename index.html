SDRAG

Structured Data Retrieval Augmented Generation

A structured approach to financial analytics that separates computation from explanation, ensuring accuracy, auditability, and executive confidence in AI-driven insights.

The Challenge

Modern AI tools can discuss numbers fluently but cannot be trusted to compute or explain financial results consistently. This creates risk, rework, and executive distrust in AI-driven analytics.

When AI systems generate both calculations and explanations, they introduce uncertainty into financial reporting—a risk that finance leaders cannot afford to take.

Inconsistent Calculations

AI systems produce varying results for the same financial metrics

Auditability Issues

Lack of transparency in how numbers are computed and explained

Executive Distrust

Finance leaders hesitate to rely on AI-generated insights

The SDRAG Approach

SDRAG introduces a structured, deterministic approach where calculations and metrics are executed by trusted analytical systems, and language models are used only to explain results after the fact.

This separation removes arithmetic hallucinations by design.

By decoupling computation from communication, we ensure that financial metrics remain accurate, consistent, and auditable while still benefiting from AI-powered insights and explanations.

Key Characteristics

Deterministic Metrics

Every calculation follows predefined rules and produces consistent results, eliminating variability in financial reporting.

Auditability & Traceability

Complete visibility into how numbers are computed and explained, with full traceability from source data to final insights.

Consistent Definitions

Standardized financial metrics that maintain meaning across analyses, ensuring organizational alignment on key performance indicators.

Human-Readable Explanations

Clear narratives that explain results without altering the underlying data, making complex financial insights accessible to all stakeholders.

Separation of Concerns

Analytics engine handles computation, AI handles communication—ensuring each component excels at what it does best.
